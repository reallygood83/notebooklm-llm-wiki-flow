# LLM Wiki priority rules

Always preserve the most decision-relevant content.

Priority order:
1. explicit obligations and prohibitions
2. retention numbers and time windows
3. student/minor safety rules
4. human review requirements for high-stakes decisions
5. enterprise controls (SSO, RBAC, audit logs, retention controls)
6. ownership, indemnity, and legal risk allocation

Do not let quiz, glossary, or generic marketing text crowd out these items.

Required outputs for major policy comparisons:
- one comparison page
- one actionable checklist page
- entity updates for each provider
- raw source capture with provenance
